Company History

ABK is a web development company that was founded in 2015 by Amir, Bereket, and Kemer. The three founders had a shared passion for technology and a vision to create a company that would provide innovative web development solutions to businesses and organizations. In the early days, ABK operated out of a small office in downtown San Francisco, with just a handful of employees. The founders worked tirelessly to build the company from the ground up, taking on any project that came their way and constantly pushing themselves to learn and grow. As ABK's reputation grew, so did the demand for their services. The company quickly expanded, hiring more developers and designers to keep up with the workload. Despite the growth, the founders remained committed to their original vision of providing high-quality, user-friendly websites that were tailored to each client's unique needs. Over the years, ABK has worked with a wide range of clients, from small startups to large corporations. The company has earned a reputation for its exceptional quality, creativity, and customer service, and has become a trusted partner for businesses looking to establish a strong online presence. Today, ABK is headquartered in the heart of Silicon Valley, with a team of experienced developers and designers who are dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends. The company continues to grow and evolve, but its commitment to providing innovative web development solutions remains as strong as ever.
